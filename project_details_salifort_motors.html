Overview

Harnessing analytics, the project aimed to boost team unity and retention in sustainable auto innovation.
Salifort Motors, a trailblazer in alternative energy vehicles, embarked on a strategic initiative to revitalize its global workforce dynamics. The journey began with a deep dive into employee data, seeking to distill insights that would fuel retention and foster a culture of innovation.
Through the power of data analytics and machine learning, pivotal insights into employee retention were unveiled, leading to the development of highly accurate predictive models and a personalized employee engagement framework that redefined workforce strategy at Salifort Motors.
